Enter The Catacombs

When a player is holding the two elemental stones in their inventory they may invite up to 3 other players in Ashford Abbey for a total party size of 4 to enter the Catacombs. You must venture to the marked location to offer the stones to a shrine of Grenth in order to enter the Presearing version of The Underworld. Some rare level 20 bosses may spawn on the way. The Charr Dhuum Legion has replaced a great amount of regular underworld enemies and bosses. Can you uncover the secrets and save Tyria?!
